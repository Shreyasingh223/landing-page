Why Choose HappyTailsAdopt?

Because we enable direct pet adoption, from one good home to another.

HappyTailsAdopt is a new digital platform with real people behind the scences. Our platform connect potential adaopters with people who need to rehome their pets. This makes it easier for good people to adopt the right pet whilst maximising the chance of pets finding their forever home.
